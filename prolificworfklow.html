Fail-safes

Hot lead alert
SMS + email alert to team lead
trigger: not contacted within 15 min
n8n monitors every hot lead. No agent activity within 15 minutes triggers emergency alert to team lead.
Stuck lead detection
Auto-trigger next follow-up
trigger: same stage > 48 hours
Lead sits in any stage 48+ hours without activity — n8n triggers next follow-up or escalates.
Zero leakage check
Emergency follow-up fires
trigger: no activity > 1 hour
New lead with zero logged activity within 1 hour triggers emergency follow-up workflow.
TCPA compliance check
Auto-block non-compliant calls
trigger: missing consent or DND flag
GHL Voice AI automatically rejects outbound calls to contacts without documented opt-in, active DND flags, or previous opt-outs. Built-in KYC and time-zone guardrails enforced.

Implementation phases

Phase 1 — Core build

Days 1–5

Pipeline setup, ReadyMode webhook integration via n8n, all GHL workflows, SMS/email sequences, Conversation AI, tagging logic, missed call text-back, contact routing.

Phase 2 — GHL Voice AI + fail-safes

Days 5–7

GHL Voice AI agent configuration (inbound, missed callback, after-hours, live transfer), n8n fail-safes, alert system, stuck lead detection, TCPA compliance verification, full testing.

Phase 3 — Optimization

Post-launch (retainer)

Monitor conversions, tune AI prompts and voice scripts, adjust sequences, refine routing for maximum deal flow.
