07

Fail-safes

Hot lead alert
SMS + email alert to team lead
trigger: not contacted within 15 min
n8n monitors every hot lead. If no agent activity within 15 minutes, emergency alert fires to team lead via SMS and email.
Stuck lead detection
Auto-trigger next follow-up
trigger: same stage > 48 hours
If a lead sits in any stage for 48+ hours without activity, n8n triggers next follow-up or escalates to team member.
Zero leakage check
Emergency follow-up fires
trigger: no activity > 1 hour
Every new lead must have a touchpoint within 1 hour. If zero activity detected, emergency follow-up fires immediately.
Workflow failure
Error log + admin alert
trigger: webhook or workflow fails
n8n monitors all connections. Failures logged with details, alert sent to admin. Auto-retry for transient failures.

Dead lead recovery loop

Dead / not interested
Removed from active sequences
Marked unresponsive after all attempts. Removed from aggressive sequences but enters the long-term nurture pool.

▼
Monthly nurture sequence

▼
