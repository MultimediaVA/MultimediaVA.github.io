Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? Here are answers to the most common ones.

What can you automate for my business?

Almost any repetitive workflow. Lead capture to CRM, client onboarding, content scheduling, email sequences, reporting, review requests, abandoned cart recovery, and more. I build on Make.com, Zapier, n8n, and GoHighLevel.

Do you set up GoHighLevel from scratch?

Yes. I handle full GHL buildouts including sub-accounts, pipelines, funnels, booking calendars, SMS/email workflows, missed-call text-back, reputation management, Stripe integration, and membership areas. I currently manage three sub-accounts as an agency admin.

What types of video editing do you offer?

YouTube content like reactions, documentaries, vlogs, and talking-head videos. Also social media reels, TikToks, UGC ads, VSLs, and D2C short-form content. I work with Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, After Effects, and CapCut.

What tools do you use for automation?

Make.com, Zapier, n8n, GoHighLevel workflows, API integrations, webhooks, and custom scripts. I also integrate with tools like Airtable, Notion, Slack, Calendly, Stripe, Meta Ads, and Google Ads.

How much does a GHL setup cost?

A full GoHighLevel CRM setup ranges from $800 to $2,500 depending on complexity. This includes pipelines, SMS/email sequences, missed-call text-back, booking funnels, reputation management, and integrations. Simpler starter setups start at $300.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. After the initial build, I offer hourly VA support at $8/hr for ongoing maintenance, updates, troubleshooting, and optimizations. Most clients keep me on retainer for 10 to 20 hours per week.

How quickly can you deliver?

Most short-form edits are delivered within 24 to 48 hours. GHL setups and automation builds typically take 3 to 7 days depending on complexity. I communicate timelines upfront and deliver on schedule.

What is your timezone?

I am based in the Philippines (GMT+8). My working hours are 4PM to 4AM Philippine time, which covers 12AM to 12PM Pacific Time. This means I am available during US morning and midday hours.

What is your experience with GoHighLevel?

I am the Agency Admin for Unity Consulting Advisors, managing three active GHL sub-accounts. I handle pipelines, funnels, workflows, lead nurture sequences, CRM integrations, and troubleshooting daily.
