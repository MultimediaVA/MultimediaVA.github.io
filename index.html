About Me

I'm Kenneth Pineda, an AI Automation Specialist, GoHighLevel Admin, and UGC Manager helping eCommerce brands, agencies, and founders scale their operations without scaling their headcount.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my automation, GHL, video editing, and virtual assistance services.

1. What can you automate for my business?

Almost any repetitive workflow. Lead capture to CRM, client onboarding, content scheduling, email sequences, reporting, review requests, abandoned cart recovery, and more. I build on Make.com, Zapier, n8n, and GoHighLevel.

2. Do you set up GoHighLevel from scratch?

Yes. I handle full GHL buildouts including sub-accounts, pipelines, funnels, booking calendars, SMS/email workflows, missed-call text-back, reputation management, Stripe integration, and membership areas. I currently manage three sub-accounts as an agency admin.

3. What types of video editing do you offer?

YouTube content like reactions, documentaries, vlogs, and talking-head videos. Also social media reels, TikToks, UGC ads, VSLs, and D2C short-form content. I work with Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, After Effects, and CapCut.

4. How do you communicate with remote clients?

I'm available via Slack, email, Zoom, and Loom. I use tools like Google Drive, Notion, Airtable, and Trello to manage projects, share files, and communicate updates in real-time.

5. How quickly can you deliver?

Most short-form edits are delivered within 24 to 48 hours. GHL setups and automation builds typically take 3 to 7 days depending on complexity. I communicate timelines upfront and deliver on schedule.
